Structure

01
Online Form Submission

The team needs to register on the Empresario website and authenticate their account with the code sent to the email provided. After that, they can log in and complete the online form.

02
Early Bird

Additional incentives include extended online mentorship and a face-to-face session for shortlisted entries. These will help improve the quality of the business models.

03
Preliminary Evaluation

After early bird registrations, entries will go to our mentor pool, which includes experts in Fintech and Agriculture. Mentors will offer feedback to refine the business models.

04
Mentorship

Shortlisted teams will receive mentoring to improve their business models. Participants should make full use of this guidance to enhance their submissions.

05
Final Submission

After mentoring, teams must submit their final Business Model in the required format. Check the link for instructions on how to prepare and submit.

06
Grand Finale

The final teams will present their Business Models to the judging panel at an event in Kolkata. Prepare thoroughly to showcase your model effectively.

Rules

Eligibility Criteria:
  1. Participants must be individuals or teams consisting of 1-5 members.
  2. Team members cannot be changed after submission.
  3. Participants may represent any academic institution or be independent entrepreneurs.
Submission Guidelines:
  1. All submissions must be in English.
  2. Failure to adhere to the timeline may result in disqualification.
Judging Criteria:
  1. Submissions will be judged based on innovation, feasibility, market potential, scalability, and the quality of the pitch.
  2. Decisions made by the judges are final and cannot be contested.
Confidentiality:
  1. All submitted materials will be treated as confidential and will only be shared with judges and competition organizers.
  2. Participants are advised not to include proprietary information unless necessary.
  3. The organizers do not claim any rights over the submitted ideas but may use them for promotional purposes with prior consent.
Code of Conduct:
  1. All participants are expected to maintain professionalism and respect towards other participants, judges, and organizers.
  2. Any form of cheating, sabotage, or unprofessional behavior will result in immediate disqualification
  3. The competition reserves the right to disqualify any submission that is deemed inappropriate or offensive.

What is Empresario?

Empresario is a premier business model competition that provides a platform for aspiring entrepreneurs to showcase their innovative ideas. It is designed to help participants refine their business concepts, receive mentorship from industry experts, and compete for top prizes.

Who can participate in Empresario?

Anyone with an innovative idea, product, service, or even an operating startup can participate. The competition is open to students, working professionals, and entrepreneurs from across the globe.

Is there a registration fee to participate in Empresario?

No, there is no fee for registration. Participation in Empresario is completely free.

How do I register for Empresario?

You can register for Empresario through our official website. Simply fill out the registration form with the required details, and you'll be entered into the competition.

Can I participate as part of a team?

Yes, you can participate as part of a team. A team can consist of a minimum of 1 and a maximum of 5 members.

What are the benefits of participating in Empresario?

Participants gain exposure to potential investors, mentorship from industry experts, and the opportunity to refine their business models. Finalists have the chance to present their ideas in front of a prestigious panel of judges and compete for top prizes.

What kind of ideas or ventures can be submitted?

You can submit any innovative idea, product, or service across various domains. However, ventures cannot be a buyout, an expansion of an existing company, a real estate syndication, a tax shelter, a franchise, a licensing agreement for distribution in a different geographical area, or a spin-out from an existing corporation.

How will my submission be evaluated?

Submissions will be evaluated based on criteria such as innovation, feasibility, market potential, and overall impact. The evaluation process includes preliminary rounds, followed by mentoring and final presentations for those who advance to the later stages.

What happens if my team is selected as a semi-finalist?

If your team is selected as a semi-finalist, you will receive mentorship from industry experts to help you refine your business model and prepare for the next stage of the competition.

Where and when will the grand finale take place?

The grand finale will take place in Kolkata on 4th February 2023. Finalists will present their business models to a panel of judges and an audience.

Where and when will the grand finale take place?

The grand finale will take place in Kolkata on 1th February 2023 (tentative). Finalists will present their business models to a panel of judges and an audience.

How can I prepare for the competition?

To prepare for the competition, we recommend thoroughly developing your business model, conducting market research, and being ready to articulate your idea clearly. Take advantage of the mentoring sessions provided to semi-finalists to refine your concept further.

Who will be the judges and mentors for the competition?

The judges and mentors for Empresario are esteemed industry professionals, successful entrepreneurs, and investors who bring a wealth of experience and knowledge to the competition.

How will I be informed about my progress in the competition?

Participants will be informed about their progress via email and updates on the official website. Semi-finalists and finalists will receive specific instructions and notifications regarding the next steps.

Empresario is a business model competition organized by IIT Kharagpur's Entrepreneurship Cell to foster and showcase innovative startup ideas.
