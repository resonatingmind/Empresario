About Empresario

Empresario is the annual business model competition organized by Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Kharagpur. Entrepreneurship Cell is searching for innovative and enterprising ideas through Empresario. Empresario'25 provides a platform for students to get excellent professional mentorship from our experienced, varied, and distinguished mentor pool, and to showcase their ideas in front of Venture Capitalists, Angel Investors, and Industry Leaders.
