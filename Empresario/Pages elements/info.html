INFO

Structure

1.Online Form Submission: The team needs to register on the Empresario website and authenticate their account using code sent to the email-id specified. After that team can log into their account and fill the form online.


2. Early Bird: Additional incentives include extended online mentorship and a face-to-face mentoring session for shortlisted entries.


3. Preliminary Evaluation: After the early bird registrations are over, we will transfer the entries to our mentor pool, which includes professionals from various fields, including Fintech and Agriculture. The mentors will give their opinion and comments on the entries, which will help the participants to brush up their business models and improve the startup itself.


4. Mentorship: Mentoring will be provided to the shortlisted teams. The participants are required to make optimum utilization of the mentoring process so as to create an appropriately refined Business Model.


5.Final Submission: After mentoring, the teams have to submit their final Business Model in a prescribed format. Visit this link to know: How to Prepare a Submission.


6.Grand Finale: The final shortlisted teams will present their final Business Model in front of the judging panel at an event to be held in Kolkata.


Early Bird

An oft-quoted term from venture capitalists is that,


"As soon as the business plan is written, it is obsolete."


Very often, teams focus on writing a business plan and never actually interact with their target customers. We believe that early on, the start-up must move quickly to prototype and test their product or service and that customer interaction is critical. Successful new ventures do not go to market with their first idea; instead, their product/ service has usually gone through several iterations before arriving at the final version.


Since Empresario is one of the few Business Model Competitions in India, we understand that our participants require assistance to generate their business model. Having the support and guidance of a mentor can be of great value to any start-up. Be it mobile technologies, social startups or even the Internet of Things, we have a pool of experienced mentors ready to assist early stage startups. We have mentors from TiE, NEN, Villgro and Totalstart, just to name a few.

Rules

1. A team may consist of minimum 1 or maximum 5 members.


2. Ventures cannot be a buyout, an expansion of an existing company, a real estate syndication, a tax shelter, a franchise, a licensing agreement for distribution in a different geographical area, or a spin-out from an existing corporation.


3. There is no restriction on any individual to participate. Anyone, from students to working professionals across the world with an idea, a product or service or even an operating startup, can participate.
